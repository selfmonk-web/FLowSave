# Privacy Policy — FlowSave

*Last updated: 2026*

---

## Summary

FlowSave does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

---

## Data storage

All data created by FlowSave — including project names, checkpoint names, saved text, notes, tags, and ratings — is stored exclusively in your browser's local IndexedDB storage. This data never leaves your device.

No data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party under any circumstances.

## Permissions

FlowSave requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Required by Chrome for the extension's local session state |
| `contextMenus` | Adds the right-click "Save as Checkpoint ✦" option when text is selected |

No other permissions are requested. FlowSave does not request access to your browsing history, cookies, tabs, or any website content beyond the text you explicitly select and save yourself.

## Third-party services

FlowSave does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The Buy Me a Coffee link in the extension header is a standard external hyperlink. Clicking it opens buymeacoffee.com in a new tab. FlowSave does not send any data to Buy Me a Coffee, and no information about your use of the extension is shared with them.

## Data deletion

To delete all data stored by FlowSave, you can either:
- Delete individual checkpoints or projects from within the extension
- Remove the extension from Chrome entirely, which clears all associated local storage

## Children's privacy

FlowSave does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including minors.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the *Last updated* date above will be updated and the change will be noted in the release notes.
